Popular Questions

How to update vessel AIS data?
Go to the Fleet Dashboard, select your vessel, and click on 'Edit AIS Parameters'. Changes usually reflect within 5-10 minutes. For bulk updates, please contact the Technical Department.
Can I post jobs anonymously?
Yes, in the Job Postings page, toggle 'Hide Company Name' before publishing. Only verified candidates who have passed the initial screen will see the specific recruiter details.
Exporting Crew Manifests for Customs
Navigate to Reports > Crewing > Manifest. You can export in PDF, Excel, or IMO standardized formats. Ensure all seafarer passport data is validated before export.
