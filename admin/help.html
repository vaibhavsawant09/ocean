Popular Questions

How to update vessel AIS data?
Go to the Fleet Dashboard, select your vessel, and click on 'Edit AIS Parameters'. Changes usually reflect within 5-10 minutes.
Can I post jobs anonymously?
Yes, in the Job Postings page, toggle 'Hide Company Name' before publishing. Only verified candidates will see the recruiter details.
